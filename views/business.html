So, what is PartyOfUs?

PartyOfUs is a restaurant waitlist app with customizable options to quickly and conveniently relay information to future patrons.

  • List your wait time
  • List space at the bar
  • Advertise drink and meal specials
  • Provide customers with info regarding handicap and child accesibility

This information will be immediately available in real-time via our site and mobile app.
